# Quick Reference: Social Media Image Sizes

## Instagram (Default Platform)
| Format | Dimensions | Ratio | Use Case |
|--------|-----------|-------|----------|
| **Post** | 1080×1080 | 1:1 | ✅ **DEFAULT** - Feed posts |
| **Story** | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | Stories, Reels |
| **Landscape** | 1080×608 | 16:9 | Horizontal photos |

## Other Platforms
| Platform | Dimensions | Ratio | Use Case |
|----------|-----------|-------|----------|
| **Facebook** | 1200×630 | 1.91:1 | Posts, links |
| **Twitter/X** | 1200×675 | 16:9 | Tweets |
| **LinkedIn** | 1200×627 | 1.91:1 | Professional posts |
| **Pinterest** | 1000×1500 | 2:3 | Pins |
| **YouTube** | 1280×720 | 16:9 | Thumbnails |
| **TikTok** | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | Video covers |

## Quick Selection Guide

### Need a Square Image?
→ **Instagram Post** (1080×1080)

### Need Vertical?
→ **Instagram Story** or **TikTok** (1080×1920)

### Need Horizontal?
→ **Twitter/X** (1200×675) or **YouTube** (1280×720)

### Need Professional?
→ **LinkedIn Post** (1200×627)

### Need Custom?
→ **Custom** (adjustable)

## Pro Tips

✅ **Start with Instagram Post** - Most versatile  
✅ **Use Story format** for mobile-first content  
✅ **YouTube thumbnails** great for presentations  
✅ **Custom sizing** for special requirements  

## Access
`/admin/developer/visuals` → Select "⭐ Stella Branded Image" → Choose resolution
